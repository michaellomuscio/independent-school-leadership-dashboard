Experimental side project. This is not a job-matching service. It's a prototype exploring how the qualitative coding of 267 position descriptions could power a different way to browse leadership searches. The tags come from direct-quote evidence in each posting — hover any match to see the exact language.

What do you want to do?

Pick action verbs that describe your ideal daily work. Positions must include every verb you select. Score reflects how strongly each verb is emphasized (primary / secondary / passing).

What kind of school character?

How important is each dimension to you? Positions must meet every threshold you set.

What situation?

Pick the kinds of situations you'd walk into. A position matches if its situation is any one of your picks (a position has only one situation type).
